## Risk Identification Checklist

### Technical Risks
- [ ] Dependencies on external systems or teams identified
- [ ] Technology constraints or limitations documented
- [ ] Performance and scalability concerns assessed
- [ ] Security vulnerabilities evaluated
- [ ] Data migration or integration risks reviewed
- [ ] Technical debt impact considered

### Resource Risks
- [ ] Team capacity and availability confirmed
- [ ] Key person dependencies identified
- [ ] Skills gaps documented with mitigation plans
- [ ] Budget constraints reviewed
- [ ] Equipment or infrastructure needs validated

### Schedule Risks
- [ ] Timeline dependencies mapped
- [ ] Critical path identified
- [ ] Buffer time allocated for high-risk activities
- [ ] External deadline constraints documented
- [ ] Release window conflicts identified

### Business Risks
- [ ] Stakeholder alignment confirmed
- [ ] Market or competitive timing risks assessed
- [ ] Regulatory or compliance requirements identified
- [ ] Customer impact evaluated
- [ ] Business continuity plans reviewed

### Communication Risks
- [ ] Stakeholder communication plan established
- [ ] Escalation paths clearly defined
- [ ] Change notification process documented
- [ ] User communication strategy prepared
- [ ] Support team readiness confirmed

## Escalation Process Checklist

### Level 1: Team-Level Escalation
- [ ] Clear criteria defined for team-level escalation
- [ ] Daily standup identified as primary forum
- [ ] Team leads empowered to resolve blockers
- [ ] Documentation process for resolved issues established

### Level 2: PM/Product Lead Escalation
- [ ] Escalation triggers clearly defined (e.g., timeline impact > 2 days)
- [ ] PM notification process documented
- [ ] Product Lead involvement criteria established
- [ ] Cross-team coordination process defined

### Level 3: Sponsor/Stakeholder Committee Escalation
- [ ] High-impact criteria documented (e.g., budget overrun, major scope change)
- [ ] Stakeholder Committee meeting schedule known
- [ ] Executive summary template prepared
- [ ] Decision turnaround time expectations set

### Special Escalations
- [ ] Security incident escalation path documented
- [ ] Data breach response procedure known
- [ ] Customer-impacting incident protocol established
- [ ] Legal or compliance escalation process defined

---

## Risk Review Cadence

### Weekly Review (PM + Team)
- [ ] Review all active risks
- [ ] Update risk status and mitigation progress
- [ ] Identify new risks from current sprint
- [ ] Close resolved risks with lessons learned

### Monthly Review (PM + Stakeholder Committee)
- [ ] Present top 3-5 risks to stakeholders
- [ ] Review escalation history and resolution time
- [ ] Assess overall risk trend (improving/worsening)
- [ ] Update risk management approach as needed

---

## Risk Mitigation Actions

For each high-priority risk:
- [ ] Specific mitigation actions defined
- [ ] Owner assigned with clear accountability
- [ ] Target completion date set
- [ ] Success criteria established
- [ ] Progress tracking mechanism in place

---

## Roles and Responsibilities

- **Project Manager**: Owns risk register, facilitates risk reviews, escalates as needed
- **Product Manager**: Assesses business impact, prioritizes risk mitigation
- **Release Manager**: Coordinates release-related risks and deployment contingencies
- **Change Manager**: Manages change-related risks and communication
- **Stakeholder Committee**: Reviews escalated risks, makes strategic decisions
- **Team Members**: Identify and report risks, implement mitigation actions
